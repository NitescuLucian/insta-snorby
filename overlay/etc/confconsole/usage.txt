Welcome to Insta-Snorby the official snobby appliance!

You may access this Insta-Snorby appliance over the
network using the following methods:

SSH/SFTP   :   root@$ipaddr (port 22)
Snorby     :   http://insta-snorby.localdomain/
Snorby (IP):   http://$ipaddr

NOTE: add "$ipaddr  insta-snorby.localdomain" to your
hosts file:
   Windows: c:\windows\system32\drivers\etc\hosts
   Linux:  /etc/hosts

For more information visit the Snorby Website
             http://www.snorby.org
